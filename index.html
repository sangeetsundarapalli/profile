Cyberthon Project - Cyber Security for Women

With the increase of technology it became more difficult to handle Cyber crimes like Cyber Harassment/Cyber Bullying, Cyber Extortion, Cyberstalking etc. This may be because of lack of awareness or fear among Women to approach Policeman or the fear about what the society thinks. Because of this many of the cyber crimes were not brought to the notice. In this Project our main goal is to protect women against Cyber violence by creating awareness and educating them about the risks in the Cyber World. We designed a website where women can file a complaint in it. You need to submit your details and explain about the Cyber-crime that got happened. The details and evidences provided will not be shared to anyone, except to the Police and will be securely stored in database. Therefore reducing the communication gap between the victim and Police. This project has been taken into consideration by Govt. of A.P and we are looking forward implementing our project.
